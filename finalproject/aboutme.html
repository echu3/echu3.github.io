I love the ocean.

Being a native Californian, the ocean feels like home to me. As cheesy as it sounds, my logo (which I made just for this project), symbolizes riding the waves, or having resilience to withstand difficult situations.

I am a jogger.

I’ve run five half marathons, one full marathon, & I’ve done the turkey trot with my family for the last six years. Biggest tip for endurance? Visualize yourself at the top of the hill you’re running on and in a matter of seconds, you’ll overcome it.

I'm a dual citizen.

As a child of Korean immigrant parents, I learned to cook the fundamentals from my mom. Making and eating korean food is a major source of comfort to me.

I enjoy reading.

I read everything from Bill Bryson to Junot Diaz to Margaret Atwood to Richard Dawkins. They say reading for just 6 minutes relieves more stress than a handful of other stress relieving activities. My top three recommendations? Pachinko by Min Jin Lee, Braiding Sweetgrass by Robin Wall Kimmerer, The Body Keeps Score by Bessel van der Kolk
